Better medicine with machine learning

Both new and long-time medical cannabis patients often struggle to identify the best strain and dosage for them, particularly when trying to find a natural alternative to pharmaceuticals, spending countless hours researching testimonials and anecdotes. There’s a better way. Using state of the art machine learning technology, MedCabinet can help determine the best options for your health.

Finding medicine that works for you

Your medical cannabis options are complex. State and local regulations on the production, sale, and use of medical cannabis differ considerably. As a result, the availablity of cannabis products varies widely. We know that this is a frequent source of frustration and confusion for patients, so MedCabinet automatically locates the nearest dispensary that carries each of your personalized recommendations.

A smarter approach to your health

Patients and providers deserve hard scientific data to guide their healthcare decisions. Federal regulations prohibit traditional clinical trials for cannabis products, and patients have long been forced to rely on word of mouth recommendations. MedCabinet brings data back into the medical cannabis conversation by carefully analyzing patients reports of the effects of different products and providing the results to patients in an easy to understand format.
